Industry-Specific Steel Building Design and Project Development

The efficiency of all indoor commercial activity is a direct function of building design.

Most commercial production facilities choose climate-controlled buildings to maximize production.

Steel buildings are the preferred solution.

Over the past 22 years, we have designed, supplied, and built over 250 steel buildings for commercial, industrial, retail, specialty, agricultural and public projects.

With the advent of the burgeoning Cannabis Industry, we have limited our work to industry-specific design and supply of pre-engineered steel buildings to any location in the U.S. and Canada, and to owner-representation in the construction process for Cannabis projects.

Our team offers the following scopes of work:

We Provide:

  • Complete steel building design for every type of cannabis business, with permit-ready plans
  • Concrete foundation engineering with permit-ready plans
  • Complete steel building packages, including freight

For The Following Industry Segments:

  • Indoor controlled-climate grows
  • Extraction facilities, volatile and non-volatile
  • Retail dispensaries, exterior design, interior design and custom fixtures, hardware and displays
  • Secure space design for "hardened rooms" and protected "rescue paths"
  • Distribution warehouses of any size and configuration
  • "Hive" warehouses, with "turn-key" partitioned units set up for lease to industry business

This scope of service provides reliable site-specific information so the client can make informed decisions pre-purchase or pre-planning.

Critical information included (partial list):

  • Utility Availability
  • Required right-of-way construction
  • Jurisdictional hurdles
  • Environmental issues
  • Soils info
  • Physical/topo limits
  • Optimal building orientation
  • Impacts of site weather patterns on building design
  • Overview of the local political climate upon development
  • Impact of proximity to market, supplies, and qualified labor upon the project
  • Future expansion and securing the right to expand
  • Related issues impacting the pre-design phase

This work reduces the amount of engineering and architectural time in generating permit-ready plans by developing the building design and floor plan ahead of drafting and civil engineering of permit documents.
